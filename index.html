About Us

Propounding the path of Bhagwan Mahavira and an ardent devotee of Shrimad Rajchandraji, Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshji is a spiritual visionary and modern-day mystic. The founder of Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur, Pujya Gurudevshri has transformed millions of lives across the globe through enlightening discourses and meditation retreats. Under His inspiration and guidance, the San Francisco Center offers regular meetings catering to different age groups and interests.

Satsang Group
This group provides an excellent opportunity for spiritual growth of seekers through the viewing of Pujya Gurudevshri’s life transforming discourses, meditation sessions, bhakti, introspection and discussions based on His teachings. Meetings are held twice a month on weekends. For more information: Raj Doshi: (650) 336-0677 | sanfrancisco@srmd.org

Youth Group
This group provides an opportunity to utilize collective group energy to absorb and implement Pujya Gurudevshri’s teachings and incorporate spirituality in their daily lives. They meet twice a month for viewing and discussing specially curated discourses for the youth, meditation, bhakti, volunteering events and other fun group activities. For more information: Parima Shah: (858) 775-1639 | sanfrancisco.youth@srmd.org

Shrimad Rajchandra Love and Care
A tenfold programme to bring joy and offer service to all living beings. This center offers regular opportunities to provide selfless service to benefit the local community in several areas including education, health and humanity. For more information: Divya Doshi: (650) 336-0677 | sanfrancisco@srlc-usa.org

The Divine Shop
To nourish your soul, avail the Mission’s various audio-video publications, books and magazines including Pujya Gurudevshri’s discourses, devotional music and children’s publications. For more information: Nishit Parekh: (408) 605-6753 | sanfrancisco@srmd.org

Raj Uphaar
supports over 200 underprivileged tribal women in their quest towards attaining self-reliance and a respectable source of income. We offer them end-to-end support by providing employment opportunities, skill training, raw material and equipment, safe workplaces, as well as product marketing and distribution. These talented and dedicated women produce more than 100 desserts, snacks, fragrances and utility products for your home.
